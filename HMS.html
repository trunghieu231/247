B2B Dynamic Packaging

Our platform aggregates a large range of travel services including hotel bookings, car rentals, activities and tours, insurance, airport transfers and many more.

What is Dynamic Packaging ?

  • Combination of different travel components which are packaged and priced in real-time in response to consumers’ demands.
  • Provides consumers to reach multiple services with a single search.
  • Provides suppliers / wholesales to hide their discount rates.
  • Consumers are able to organize their own travel packages through dynamic packaging.

Dynamic Packaging Features

Inventory Control

  • Provide inventories from various travel components; eg: airline, hotel, transfer, activities, and etc.
  • Client has limited control in type / category of inventory
  • Client has limited control from inventory Intelligence Ranking

Conversion Optimization

  • Smart Tracking to track users activities at throughout customer journey
  • Retargeting for potential customer to assist them to complete the booking
  • Email to potential customers to explore new travel idea or assist in completing a booking
